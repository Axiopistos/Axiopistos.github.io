Business Sector: Power & Electric Infrastructure (PEI)

Power & Electric Infrastructure (PEI) division is engaged in machinery, equipment & components required in the electric power plants including renewable energy segment (solar, wind, hydro & others) and its transmission & distribution network.

We provide support in the low voltage, medium voltage & high voltage (including EHV) segment for various products & project solutions listed below but not limited to:
• Power Transformer
• Distribution Transformer (including Dry Type Transformer)
• Reactor
• Gas Insulated Switchgear
• Power Cables & Conductors
• Circuit Breaker
• Surge Arrestor
• Control Panel
• Solar Panel
• Inverters
• Turbines
• Alternators
• Diesel Generator Set
• Compact Substation & other products
