ABOUT

We, Axiopistos Associates Private Limited (AXPS) are a company based in Hyderabad, India which is into providing single-point marketing, consulting & sourcing solutions to our esteemed Clients & Partner/Associate Vendors located in India and overseas market.

With a resolve to be a true partner in the progress of our Clients & Partner/Associate Vendors, we serve our Clients for their product/project requirements by sourcing & procuring the best suited products & solutions from our Partner/Associate Vendors based on the technical & economic viability which meets the requirement of the Clients/End User.

We connect our Clients with Partner/Associate Vendors & other prospective suppliers for the products/projects and develop a long-lasting business relation between all stakeholders.

Our business focuses on the following:
• Power & Electric Infrastructure
• Industrial Systems & Building Facilities
• Renewable Energy
