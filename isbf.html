Business Sector: Industrial Systems & Building Facilities

Axiopistos Associates' Industrial Systems & Building Facilities division is dedicated to revolutionizing the industrial and building sectors through innovative consulting, marketing, and sourcing solutions. Our focus is on enhancing productivity in industrial setups and advancing facility management systems. We are committed to providing state-of-the-art services that exceed the evolving demands of modern industries and building infrastructures.

We offer a comprehensive range of services designed to enhance efficiency, robustness, and technological advancement in various systems and facilities:

CNC Machines: Precision machinery designed for high-performance manufacturing, offering unparalleled accuracy and efficiency.
Process Automation (PLC, SCADA, DCS): Advanced automation solutions to streamline industrial processes, enhancing productivity and reducing operational costs.
ERP Systems: Comprehensive Enterprise Resource Planning systems tailored to integrate and manage core business processes, driving efficiency and decision-making.
IT Storage Systems: Robust and scalable IT storage solutions to safeguard critical data and ensure seamless business operations.
Building Management Systems: Intelligent building management solutions for enhanced control over building operations, ensuring energy efficiency and optimized performance.
CCTV Systems: State-of-the-art surveillance systems providing top-tier security and monitoring capabilities.
Access Control Systems: Advanced access control solutions to ensure secure and regulated entry into facilities.
Audio & Visual Equipment: High-quality audio-visual systems for effective communication and immersive experiences in corporate and commercial spaces.
UPS & Other Power Solutions: Uninterruptible Power Supply systems and related products to ensure continuous and reliable power supply, critical for uninterrupted operations.

At Axiopistos Associates, we are committed to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in industrial systems and building facilities. Our team collaborates closely with clients to provide tailored solutions that are not only innovative but also sustainable and environmentally conscious.

Our aim is to lead the revolution in industrial systems and building facilities. We continuously explore new technologies and approaches, ensuring that our clients benefit from the most advanced and effective solutions available. Join us as we pave the way for a smarter, more efficient future in these vital sectors.
